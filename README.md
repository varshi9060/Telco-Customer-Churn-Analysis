## 📌 What is this project about?
Customer retention is one of the biggest challenges in the telecom industry. In this project, I took a raw customer dataset, loaded it into MySQL to dig out structural insights, and built a fully interactive Power BI dashboard to show exactly why customers are leaving and where the business is losing money.

## 💻 Tools I Used
* **SQL (MySQL Workbench):** For writing analytical queries, filtering target audiences, and data profiling.
* **Power BI Desktop:** For data modeling, building business KPI metrics, and designing the final interactive report.

## 💡 Advanced Data Discoveries (Deep-Dive Analysis)
* **The 18-Month Critical Window:** Our SQL average tenure query proved that churning customers leave at an average of 18 months. If we can retain a customer past the 1.5-year mark, their loyalty increases significantly.
* **The Single-User Vulnerability:** Demographics profiling revealed that customers without dependents (Single users/Bachelors) are 5 times more likely to churn compared to family plans. Single users have low brand stickiness.
* **Financial Drag:** Looking at the overall impact, early-stage churn is causing a severe dent in profitability, as the high customer acquisition cost (marketing/setup) isn't being recovered before they leave.

*(Note: Major structural drivers like Month-to-Month contracts and Electronic Check issues are fully mapped out with live tracking numbers inside the visual layout).*
